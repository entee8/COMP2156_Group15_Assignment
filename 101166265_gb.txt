George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college located in Toronto, Ontario.
It offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and certificate programs in areas such as business, health sciences, technology, arts, and hospitality.
There are three main campuses in Toronto: 
	1) St. James Campus: 200 King Street East, Toronto, ON M5A 3W8
	2) Casa Loma Campus: 160 Kendal Avenue, Toronto, ON M5R 1M3
	3) Waterfront Campus: 51 Dockside Drive, Toronto, ON M5A 0B6
